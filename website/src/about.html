WRITER | COPYWRITER | EDITOR

I'm a journalist, editor, and copywriter with an expertise in travel and outdoor adventure.

I've written for national magazines and websites and created content for leading travel and outdoor brands. My work has appeared in Travel + Leisure, VICE, and Outside, and I've collaborated with brands like KAYAK, Fairmont, and the Telluride Tourism Board. I have a degree in journalism and marketing from the University of Denver and attended the NYU Publishing Institute.

Thanks to some tried-and-true travel hacks (and a career that lets me work from anywhere), I've explored over 55 countries. I've skied the Japanese Alps while living out of a camper van, navigated the Philippines with a toddler in tow, and studied French in Paris. I'm happiest en route to somewhere new or exploring on skis.
